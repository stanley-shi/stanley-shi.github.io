Question for Module 1:

1. What are the two categories of data? Structured and un-structured data;

2. what are the five core technology elements of the Data Center Infrastructure? Applications, Databases, Server and Operating Systems, Network devices, and Storage Arrays;

2.5 Four part of the Data Center Infrastructure: Computer Storage System, network device, dedicated power backup system, environmental control system.

3. What are the seven requirements of storage technology? Data Integrity, Availability, Security, Performance, Scalability, Capacity, Manageability.

4. What are the benefits of ILM? Improved utilization, Simplified management, Simplified backup and recovery, Maintaining compliance, Lower Total Cost of Ownership
